Ongoing Research · UC Riverside
Physics-Informed Multi-Modal GNN for Molecular Solubility Jan 2025 – Present
Advisor: Prof. Jianzhong Wu · UC Riverside

Architected a multi-modal fusion GNN (~5.7M parameters, 5-fold ensemble) combining AttentiveFP solute encoder, GIN solvent encoder, and descriptor MLP. Embedded the Van't Hoff thermodynamic constraint as a physics-informed auxiliary loss. Achieved R²=0.968, RMSE=0.244 on held-out test set. Implemented GradCAM-based solvent-aware atomic attribution.

Multi-objective Optimization of Ethylene Cracking Furnaces Jan 2025 – Present
2nd Author · Advisors: Prof. Du Wenli & Prof. Zhao Liang · ECUST

Developed a multi-objective MINLP optimization framework balancing economic efficiency and carbon emissions in petrochemical systems. Achieved 38.6% CO₂ reduction via dynamic coking mitigation. ML-based surrogate models handle coke thickness prediction and heat transfer optimization across multi-furnace systems.
