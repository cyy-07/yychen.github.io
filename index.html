03 — HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. One clear answer.

01
Input Reality
Courses, assignments, exams, schedules, and life events — all in one place. Upload files or use the quick planner.
02
Model Uncertainty
Tasks become ranges, not fixed durations. Every item carries a min, max, and confidence level to reflect real life.
03
Simulate Futures
The engine generates Plan A, B, and C — showing stress trajectories, feasibility scores, and what to cut.
