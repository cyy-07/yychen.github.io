I’m Yiyang Chen, a final-year student我叫陈祎洋，是一名大四学生 with a dual background in Chemical Engineering (ECUST, Shanghai) and Computer Science & AI (UC Riverside). I grew up in Fujian, China, ranked in the top 3% of 218,000 students in the college entrance exam — but what matters more to me is the direction I’ve been building since.在华东理工大学主修化学工程，同时在加州大学河滨分校修读计算机科学与AI。成长于福建，高考在21.8万考生中位列前3%——但对我而言更重要的，是此后一直在构建的方向。

Outside research, I like hiking (hence the tent button 🏕), stargazing, and occasionally reminding myself that rest is productive. FLUXMIND
— OVERLOAD RADAR
Cognitive Load Simulation System & Decision Engine认知负载模拟系统与决策引擎

FluxMind is a cognitive load simulator that models how stress accumulates across competing life decisions. Given your schedule, exams, and life events, it runs three parallel “what-if” futures — simulating stress trajectories over time using a probabilistic uncertainty engine and a constraint-based decision solver. The goal: not to tell you what to do, but to make the invisible cognitive cost of choices visible before you commit to them.FluxMind 是一个认知负载模拟器，模拟压力在相互竞争的生活决策中如何积累。输入你的日程、考试和生活事件，它会运行三条并行的"假设"未来——通过概率性不确定性引擎和约束决策求解器模拟压力轨迹。目标不是告诉你该怎么做，而是让选择背后隐形的认知代价在你做决定之前变得可见。

EcoLung
Personal Exposure & Sustainable Action Simulator个人暴露量与可持续行动模拟器

EcoLung turns invisible long-term air pollution exposure into a visible, personal story. Users select a real location, define their current daily routine, and compare it to a better one — then watch the difference play out on an interactive 3D lung that changes color, particle density, and airflow over a continuous year timeline. The app pulls live environmental data from OpenAQ and uses a behavior-parameterized exposure engine to project long-term respiratory burden. Not a dashboard. Not a diagnostic. A simulator that makes the invisible visible.EcoLung 将无形的长期空气污染暴露转化为可感知的个人故事。用户选择真实地点、设定当前日常习惯，与改善后的习惯对比——在交互式3D肺部模型上实时看到差异，颜色、粒子密度和气流随着年份时间轴动态演变。应用从OpenAQ获取实时环境数据，通过行为参数化暴露引擎预测长期呼吸负担。不是仪表板，不是诊断工具，而是让隐形可见的模拟器。
